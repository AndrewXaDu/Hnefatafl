Hnefatafl

Viking Chess - 11x11

Defender's Turn

Rules

  • - Defender: Move King to a corner to win
  • - Attacker: Surround the King to win
  • - All pieces move like rooks
  • - Cannot jump over pieces
  • - Warriors cannot stop on center/corners
  • - Pinch capture: sandwich enemy warrior
  • - Restricted squares act as pinch walls
  • - Surround capture: 4 pieces around King

Piece Count

Defender: 13
Attacker: 24
